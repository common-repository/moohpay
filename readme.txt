Official MoohPay™ WordPress Plugin. Connects to your MoohPay™ account and enables you to sell digital products with ease.

== Description ==

**To use this plugin you need a [free MoohPay™ account](https://www.moohpay.com/join).**

Official MoohPay™ WordPress Plugin. Sell digital products like a cowboy.

* Use this plugin to instantly connect to your MoohPay™ account.
* You then get a full list with all your products.
* Just pick one and it is displayed beautifully.
* You can also lock pages and grant access to customers who bought a specific product only.
* Easily sell your online courses, make easy premium video access pages, and let MoohPay™ handle the authentication for you.
* **This plugin is fully translated into English and German.**

You can add products to posts, pages and widget sections, like a sidebar or footer.

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the MoohPay™ plugin and get it working.

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/plugin-name` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly by entering "moohpay" into the search field.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
3. Use the MoohPay™ screen to connect with your MoohPay™ account.
